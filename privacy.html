Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2026

Welcome to CU CROSSING. Your privacy and safety are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your data protection.

1. Zero Data Collection

CU CROSSING is designed as a standalone safety tool. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, phone number, email address, IP address, or device identifiers.

2. No Location Tracking

We believe your movements are your own business. CU CROSSING does not use GPS or any other location-tracking technologies. We do not know where you are or where you are going.

3. Camera & Flashlight Access

To provide maximum visibility at night, the App requires access to your device's Flashlight (LED). Under system requirements, this is categorized as "Camera Access." We DO NOT use the camera to take photos, record videos, or capture any visual data. The access is strictly limited to controlling the LED light for safety signaling.

4. Offline Operation

All core functions of CU CROSSING operate entirely on your local device. No data is transmitted to external servers while you are using the safety light features.

5. Third-Party Services

This App does not integrate any third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. You can use CU CROSSING with complete peace of mind, knowing that no "trackers" are following your activity.

6. Contact Us
